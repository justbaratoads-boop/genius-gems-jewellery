Privacy Policy

Home  /  Privacy Policy

1. Commitment to Privacy

At Genius Gems & Jewellery, we hold your trust in high regard. We are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of any personal details you share with us while browsing our loose gemstone collections or using our bespoke design features.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you interact with our website. This includes:

  • Bespoke Order Inquiries: Your name, WhatsApp phone number, email address, gemstone preferences, estimated budget, and description of your design vision.
  • Contact Inquiries: Details submitted through our contact form (name, email, subject, message).
  • Privilege Newsletter: Email address provided during newsletter signup.
  • Wishlist & Cart State: Selected gemstones saved on your browser's local storage (localStorage) for ease of browsing.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the details collected to deliver a premium, personalized gemstone consulting experience. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Process your custom design inquiries and coordinate with our gemstone cutters.
  • Contact you via WhatsApp or email to share CAD sketches, gemstone certificates, and price quotes.
  • Fulfill newsletter updates if you choose to subscribe.
  • Optimize our web catalog performance and responsiveness.

4. Data Storage & WhatsApp Transfers

To maximize privacy, Genius Gems & Jewellery does not store your custom inquiry form submissions on permanent database servers. Instead:

  • Form details are compiled into a structured message template and transferred directly to our secure WhatsApp line via official WhatsApp chat APIs when you submit.
  • Wishlist selections are kept locally on your own computer/device via browser cookies/localStorage and are never stored on our side.

5. Security of Information

We implement appropriate technical measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your transfer records. However, please note that no internet transmission is 100% secure, and communications sent over WhatsApp are subject to WhatsApp's separate security frameworks.

6. Your Choices & Rights

You can manage your local browser wishlist data at any time by clearing your browser cache. If you have subscribed to our Privilege newsletter and wish to unsubscribe, you can do so by contacting us at our email address listed below.
