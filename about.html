Legacy of Purity & Natural Brilliance

Founded in 1998 in the historic gems district of Mumbai, Genius Gems & Jewellery began with a singular vision: to curate and cut natural gemstones that transcend generations. Over nearly three decades, we have remained committed to this vision, combining traditional gem-cutting artistry with modern gemological testing standards.

Our journey is built on the pillars of certified purity, exceptional gemstone sourcing, and transparent customer trust. Every gemstone in our collection carries independent laboratory verification, detailing origin, weight, and treatment status, ensuring your investment is authentic and valuable.

At Genius Gems, we partner with you in curating precious natural assets. Whether you are looking for an astrological Yellow Sapphire, a collector-grade Pigeon Blood Ruby, or a custom engagement Solitaire Diamond, we ensure every cut facet captures light and value perfectly.

Our Craftsmanship Philosophy

The standard of excellence we pledge to every patron

Handmade with Devotion

While technology aids precision, the soul of our jewelry lies in handcrafted details. Our experienced karigars (artisans) preserve ancient techniques like Kundan setting, Nakshi carving, and filigree work.

Precision Diamond Sourcing

Only 1 in 100 diamonds passes our selection process. We inspect color, fluorescence, clarity, and dimensions beyond standard laboratory reports to ensure exceptional light reflection.

Uncompromising Honesty

We believe in transparent billing. Detailed metal weights, stone carats, and labor charges are explicitly laid out. Our lifetime buyback offers are the most straightforward in the industry.

Guarantees of Authenticity

Third-party seals certifying our unmatched standards

BIS 916 Hallmark

Government of India standard certifying the purity of our 22 Karat gold jewellery, tested at authorized assaying centers.

GIA Certification

The Gemological Institute of America's independent certification detailing specifications for our solitaire diamonds.

IGI Grading

International Gemological Institute certificate for diamond studs and necklaces, detailing color grade and clarity.

HUID Traceability

Every gold ornament features a unique Hallmarking Unique ID (HUID) laser stamp, allowing traceability of pure metal origin.
